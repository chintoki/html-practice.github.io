Gintoki Sakata

Role: Main Character

Debut: Episode 3 (Official)

Affiliation: Yorozuya

Occupation: Leader, Odd Jobs (Yorozuya)

Is the main protagonist of the Gintama series. He is the founder and president of the Yorozuya, as well as a highly-skilled samurai, having fought in the Joui War in the past. During the war, he became known as the "White Yaksha" due to his powerful swordsmanship, demonic appearance, white clothing, and silver hair.

Sougo Okita

Role: Supporting Character

Debut: Episode 5 (Official)

Affiliation: Shinsengumi

Occupation: 1st Division Captain, Shinsengumi

Is the 1st Division Captain of the Shinsengumi and was a trainee at Isao Kondou's dojo in his youth. He is the younger brother of Mitsuba Okita who raised him after their parents passed away. He is recognized as the most talented swordsman in the Shinsengumi and takes pride in his job to lead the vanguard as to open a path for the Shinsengumi.

Shinpachi Shimura

Role: Main Character

Debut: Episode 3 (Official)

Affiliation: Yorozuya

Occupation: Member, Odd Jobs (Yorozuya)

Is a member of the Yorozuya, a samurai in training, and one of the three main protagonists of Gintama. He is one of the more normal characters of the series, delivering the requisite reality-checks or punchlines against other characters' antics
