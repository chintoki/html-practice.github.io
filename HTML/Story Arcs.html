Courtesan of a Nation Arc

Ratings: 10/10

Episodes: 257-261

Arc Protagonist: Gintoki Sakata, Tsukuyo, Kagura, Shinpachi, Nobume, Rotten Maizou, Suzuran

Gintoki is invited to Yoshiwara to meet Suzuran, once the most beautiful courtesan, as a thank you for saving Yoshiwara. However, he is shocked to find that she is now an elderly prostitute, still waiting for a man she loved who promised to meet her on the next full moon. Mistaking Gintoki for him, Suzuran wraps a strand of hair around his pinkie. Gintoki and Tsukuyo then decide to help Suzuran find the man she's been waiting for.

- You will never awaken from this dream. We will always, always...be together. I promise.

(Rotten Maizou to Suzuran. Who's cutting onions?!)

Shinsengumi Crisis Arc

Ratings: 9.8/10

Episodes: 101-105

Arc Protagonist: Hijikata Toushirou, Kondo Isao, Sougo Okita

Hijikata receives a cursed sword that causes him to act strangely, putting his position in danger. At the same time, Shinsengumi adviser Itou Kamotarou returns to the Shinsengumi to push it forward, but Hijikata suspects something more is going on.

- Who am I? I am the Shinsengumi Vice-Commander, Hijikata Toushirou!

(Hijikata to Shinsengumi Members. Best Arc involving the Shinsengumi so far!)

Yoshiwara in Flames Arc

Ratings: 9.5/10

Episodes: 139-146

Arc Protagonist: Gintoki Sakata, Kagura, Shinpachi, Tsukuyo, Hinowa, Seita

Yoshiwara is an underground red-light district governed by different laws. Gintoki bumps into a pickpocketing kid that needs money to buy a woman who he believes to be his mother...

- You can't blow out my flame. As long as I'm here, I'll burst back into flames every time you blow them out.

(Gintoki to Houzen. 2nd Best Arc in the Yoshiwara Trilogy)

Kintama Arc

Ratings: 9.4/10

Episodes: 253-256

Arc Protagonist: Gintoki Sakata, Tama, Sadaharu

Gintoki's Identity has been stolen by Kintoki, an android made by Gengai as an assistant for Shinpachi and Kagura since Gin is always ditching them. Kintoki essentially becomes a perfect replacement of Gin. Everyone has come to adore the new Yorozuya boss, whilst seemingly forgetting Gintoki...

- Listen up, Kintoki. The main character in Gintama is every last idiot alive in this show!

(Gintoki to Kintoki)

Red Spider Arc

Ratings: 9.1/10

Episodes: 177-181

Arc Protagonist: Tsukuyo, Gintoki Sakata

An unknown man is seen overlooking Yoshiwara from a window explaining to a courtesan about the "Moon" of Yoshiwara. A flashback of Tsukuyo as a child creating the scars on her face is shown. She is being told by a man to give up her womanhood because he will be her sole protector.

- Your face isn't ugly. It's a pretty face carrying a clean soul.

(Gintoki to Tsukuyo. ❤ Best Ship in the Series!. Weakest Yoshiwara Trilogy Arc)

Kabukicho Four Devas Arc

Ratings: 9.1/10

Episodes: 210-214

Arc Protagonist: Gintoki Sakata, Kagura, Shinpachi

A girl named Pirako Chin is looking for the strongest man in the Kabuki district, and came to Gintoki to join the Yorozuya. The Yorozuya were hesitant to recruit her, especially after learning that she was a bloodthirsty gangster who wants to help Gintoki rule the Kabuki District.

- Don't die...
- until I kill you...

(Gintoki and Jirocho to each other)

Yagyuu Arc

Episodes: 76-81

Arc Protagonist: Shinpachi Shimura

Kondo is being put through an arranged marriage... to a gorilla!? The Shinsengumi are pleading to Otae for help, but an old friend of Otae's returns to claim her as their bride instead...

- Someone who makes his or her beloved cry...isn't a man or a woman. It's called a jerk!

(Shinpachi and Gintoki side by side to Kyuubei and Binbokusai)
